# Boycotts and Consumer Pressure

[Boycotts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boycott) are a form of [nonviolent action](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nonviolent_action) that use economic pressure to change behavior. When well targeted, boycotts can influence corporate policy, investor decisions, and public narratives.
